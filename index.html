our story

Enjoy Every Moment with Soulful, Home Cooked Catering

Lisa’s Catering is a family-run kitchen bringing warm, home-style flavors to your events. Think crispy flavored wings, creamy mac & cheese, oven-baked chicken, green beans, and sweet finishes like mini bundt cakes, brownies, and peach cobbler. We focus on freshness, presentation, and portions that make guests feel taken care of.
